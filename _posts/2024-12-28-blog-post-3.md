Liping Zhang, a member of CLAIR, presented two papers at the [33rd ISMRM Annual Meeting](https://www.ismrm.org/24m/), held in Singapore from May 4 to 9, 2024. The first, *“CAMP-Net: The Application of Consistency-Aware Multi-Prior in Deep Learning for Rapid MRI,”* was delivered as an oral presentation, while the second, *“A Self-Consistency-Guided Multi-Prior Deep Learning Framework for Reconstruction of Fast Spatiotemporal MRI and Its Applications in Cardiac MRI,”* was presented as an e-poster.
