Our [CLAIR lab](https://www.diir.cuhk.edu.hk/research/artificial-intelligence/) presented multiple papers at the 17th International Conference on Biomedical Engineering (ICBME) in Singapore, which took place from December 9-12, 2019. **Yongcheng Yao** presented one paper: “Brain Activity Indexing via Network Clustering” as an oral presentation. **Liping Zhang** presented one paper: “LPAQR-Net: Lightweight Pyramid Attention Quick Refinement Network for Automatic Whole-Spine Vertebrae Segmentation from Lateral Radiographs of Patients with Adolescent Idiopathic Scoliosis” as a poster presentation.
