Liping Zhang, a member of CLAIR, delivered an invited talk at the [Statistical Atlases and Computational Modeling of the Heart (STACOM) Workshop](https://stacom.github.io/stacom2023/), held in conjunction with the International Conference on Medical Image Computing and Computer-Assisted Intervention (MICCAI) 2023 in Vancouver, Canada, from October 8 to 12, 2023. The presentation, titled *“k-t CLAIR: Self-Consistency Guided Multi-Prior Learning for Dynamic Parallel MR Image Reconstruction,”* was invited following the work’s recognition as a Top 3 winner in both tasks of the CMRxRecon 2023 Challenge, which attracted more than 200 teams and over 600 participants from 22 countries and regions worldwide.
